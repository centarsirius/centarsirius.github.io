Tutoring

About:

At Princeton, I took up several opportunities to tutor students: I designed a short cosmology class for the Princeton SPLASH program and helped lead a short introductory class on Bulgarian along with several other Bulgarians during Wintersession. My first summer I also tutored math and physics at a local summer school in Plainsboro, NJ, where I prepared gifted students for the SAT and ACT standardized tests. I am also listed as a tutor on the Tutors against Covid project, founded by a fellow graduate student.
